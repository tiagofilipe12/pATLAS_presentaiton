Plasmids

  • Extrachromosomal linear or circular DNA
    • Mosaicism (between strains, species and genera)
    • Highly modular and chimeric

Plasmids

  • Mobile Elements
  • Key components in horizontal gene transfer
    • They often carry genes that may benefit the survival of the organism (e.g antibiotic resistance)
